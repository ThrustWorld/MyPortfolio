Dirty City

Platform game about environmental pollution, created for European Integrity Games.

You're an anti-hero who participates in the pollution of the planet. Along your journey, you will spread waste everywhere you go.
You will discover that your actions finally have consequences.

I handled:

- UI for menus and credits;

- Systems for player input and audio;

- Animations for the player and the interactions with the objects in the game.

Tools: C# - Unity
