Nightmare Before Eco Congress

A serious game based on social and environmental issues through lobbying mechanisms, created for European Integrity Games.

You will experience the nightmare of a mayor invited to a wind turbine
congress in a large and almost deserted hotel. You will have to make a final decision on whether to install wind turbines
in your village. You will have to make choices despite the confusion of influences and lobbies.

I collaborated on:

- The systems and the managers of the game (Audio, Options, Save, ...);

- Gameplay programming for interactions, minigames and collisions.

- UI for the HUD, popups, menus and credits;

Tools: C# - Unity
